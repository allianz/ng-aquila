<nx-checkbox> Default<br> The box is left unchecked. </nx-checkbox> <nx-checkbox [checked]="true"> Checked<br> An action or item is selected. </nx-checkbox> <nx-checkbox [disabled]="true"> Disabled<br> The checkbox is inactive due to previously checked or unchecked boxes. </nx-checkbox> <nx-checkbox [checked]="true" [disabled]="true"> Checked & disabled<br> An action or item has been pre-selected due to previously checked or unchecked boxes. </nx-checkbox> <nx-checkbox [indeterminate]="true"> Indeterminate<br> If all subordinate boxes are checked, the "mother checkbox" may be as well. But if only _some_ of them are, it's state is indeterminate, i.e. partially checked. This may come in handy if you work with nested checkboxes. </nx-checkbox> <nx-checkbox [indeterminate]="true" [disabled]="true"> Indeterminate & disabled<br> The box is indeterminate and can not be interacted with directly, but only via checking all of the subordinate boxes nested below. </nx-checkbox>
